Deutsch-Daily is a vibe coding experiment using Claude to create the whole thing as a Progressive Web App (PWA).
The initial app was done on an iPhone in around 90 minutes using Claude and Github to serve the pages in an airline lounge in MSP (Minnesota) while waiting for my flight!

Each lesson consists of two nouns, a verb, an adjective and two sentences to illustrate those items.

Each Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR) level, A1, A2 etc. is presented as a set of 25 lessons grouped into blocks of five. 

The vocabulary in the lessons at each CEFR level is presented as follows

| Level | Nouns | Verbs | Adjs | Sentences | Total words/lesson     | Total across 25 lessons  |
|-------|-------|-------|------|-----------|------------------------|--------------------------|
| A1    | 2     | 1     | 1    | 2         | 4 vocab + 2 sentences  | 100 vocab + 50 sentences |
| A2    | 3     | 2     | 2    | 2         | 7 vocab + 2 sentences  | 175 vocab + 50 sentences |
| B1    | 4     | 3     | 3    | 2         | 10 vocab + 2 sentences | 250 vocab + 50 sentences |
| B2    | 5     | 4     | 4    | 2         | 13 vocab + 2 sentences | 325 vocab + 50 sentences |
| C1    | 5     | 4     | 4    | 2         | 13 vocab + 2 sentences | 325 vocab + 50 sentences |
| C2    | 5     | 4     | 4    | 2         | 13 vocab + 2 sentences | 325 vocab + 50 sentences |

C1 and C2 have the same structure as B2 — the complexity comes from the register and specialisation of the vocabulary rather than the quantity.


After each block of 5 lessons there is a short test consisting of twelve questions on the contents of the 5 lessons in that block. 
The block test is broken down into:

3 noun translations
2 verb translations
1 adjective translation
2 sentence translations (German → English)
4 article questions (der / die / das)

Pass rate is 90% so you have to get 11 or 12 correct to pass.

After completing all five block tests in the CEFT level, there is a final level exam of 40 questions which consists of:

14 noun translations
8 verb translations
6 adjective translations
6 sentence translations
6 article questions

Drawn from across all 25 lessons in the level. Requires 90% correct to pass.
After that you can proceed to the next CEFR level.
